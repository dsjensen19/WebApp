Find your Group!

Whether you play in board game cafe’s or on a ping-pong table in someone’s den, your group of boardgamers is critical. However, not everyone is looking for the same things in their board game group. Some people look for cutthroat competition, and others for casual camaraderie. Be sure to fill out your board gamer profile so people know what you are looking for in your gaming, and don’t forget to look at other’s profile too!

Profile

When filling out your profile, here’s what you need: Your name, your zip code, your favorite board games, your preferred style of play (competitive or casual), and anything else you want to put in there. Let people know if you are willing to host games, and if you are down for online play.
