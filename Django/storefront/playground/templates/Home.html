Welcome to the Bored Gamer!

The Bored Gamer is the perfect website for anyone looking to play some of their favorite board games. Have you ever found the coolest board game ever, but then not had anyone to play it with? Just like you, thousands of other board gamers face the same problem, and some of them likely live in your hometown. This website helps connect these board gamers together. Finding your Board Game

The first step is to find your board game. Here on this site, we have dozens of our favorite board games to introduce you to, along with reviews made by fans of the game. Find your next favorite game and a community to play it with, all on our helpful Favorite Games page.

Finding Your Group

After finding your game, find a group to play it with. Visit our Group Finder page and put in your zip code to find all of the bored gamers around you. Our networks are always growing! Be sure to fill out a Bored Gamer Profile so people know if you like to play competitively or casual. Fill out your profile with your favorite genres and games.

Board Game of the Week

Twilight Imperium (Fourth Edition) is a game of galactic conquest in which three to six players take on the role of one of seventeen factions vying for galactic domination through military might, political maneuvering, and economic bargaining. Every faction offers a completely different play experience, from the wormhole-hopping Ghosts of Creuss to the Emirates of Hacan, masters of trade and economics. These seventeen races are offered many paths to victory, but only one may sit upon the throne of Mecatol Rex as the new masters of the galaxy.
